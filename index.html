Frequently Asked Questions

How do you price jobs?

We provide transparent, upfront pricing based on the scope of work, materials needed, and time required. Every estimate includes detailed breakdowns with no hidden fees.

Do you provide same-day service?

We offer same-day service for emergency situations when possible. For most services, we schedule within 24-48 hours to ensure quality work.

What items do you not remove?

We cannot remove hazardous materials, chemicals, paint, or items requiring special disposal permits. We'll discuss any restrictions during your free estimate.

Are you insured and licensed?

Yes, we are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We carry comprehensive liability insurance to protect your property and our team.

What happens to removed items?

We prioritize recycling and donation whenever possible. Usable items go to local charities, recyclables are properly processed, and only true waste goes to landfills.
